Controls:

Mouse - Click and drag to rotate mesh 1 or 2

Keyboard:
	1 - Select mesh 1
	2 - Select mesh 2
	
	3 - Toggle Directional light
	4 - Toggle Spotlight
	5 - Toggle Point light (moving)
	6 - Toggle Ambient light
	
	Arrow keys - Rotate camera
	Page Up - Pan camera out
	Page Down - Zoom camera in
